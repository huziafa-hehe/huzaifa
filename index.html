HomeCloud

// locally hosted backup system · self-sovereign data

A fully self-hosted backup application built from the ground up. HomeCloud gives you complete control over your data — no third-party cloud, no subscriptions, no privacy compromises. Run it on your own hardware and back up what matters, on your terms.

🔒
Private by Design
Your data never leaves your network. Full local control.
⚡
Fast & Lightweight
Optimized Kotlin backend keeps resource usage minimal.
🐍
Python Automation
Scripted backup jobs, scheduling, and restore workflows.
🏠
Self-Hosted
Runs on your own server — TrueNAS, Raspberry Pi, anything.
